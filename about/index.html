ABOUT THE SITE


This site seeks to provide a more compact and user-friendly version of the ZZZ Shiyu Defense & Deadly Assault history.


I may get extremely busy at times, but I try to update the site as soon as new info/suggestions drop!




This is a project solely made and maintained by me.


I wish I had some sort of access to an API (or even knew how to use it) but I didn't.


So, I did this all manually with a lot of scripts to help compile the data together. (haha...)




And as you may notice, this site is heavily based around the websites hakush.in & homdgcat.wiki, as I have really liked both of their formatting.


The enemy counts and info are all based on ZZZ official data, but some of the colors come from their websites because I found them nice to have.
