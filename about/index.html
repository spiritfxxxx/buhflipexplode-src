ABOUT ME


Hello! I am 19 years old, and am currently a sophomore studying Computer Science at university.


My online name is spiritfx, and you can almost always find me on Discord (@fx69).


I used to play an assortment of games (Minecraft, Battle Cats, Genshin Impact, Honkai Star Rail, Chaos Zero Nightmare, etc.)


Now, Zenless Zone Zero is the only game I play.


WEBSITE HISTORY


I started looking into ZZZ leaks around Version 1.4.


Much later around Version 2.0, I joined a Discord server that enjoys leaks & theorycrafting.


The members really liked endgame, and frequently referenced this one spreadsheet for HP values.


I wanted to make a cleaner, more detailed, and nicely formatted version of that spreadsheet.


So, the first iteration (deprecated) of a ZZZ endgame archive was made.




After about a month of constantly editing the sheet, it got tedious.


Also, I noticed the lack of a place that stored various ZZZ related tools.


I wanted something automatic, fast, and condensed.


That was when the journey of this website's development began.




In the middle of development, GachaBase was announced.


I was afraid it would deprecate my website before I even pushed it out.


So, sometimes I wouldn't sleep until 4 AM to do programming/formatting.


I eventually published the first "full" version to the public.


It was missing a lot, but had plenty of features I thought people would like to have.


But now, everything that's needed should be implemented!


I'm still sort of glad I beat them to it though. (I'm safe for now...)
