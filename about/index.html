WEBSITE HISTORY


Around ZZZ's 2.0 patch, I joined a Discord server that enjoyed leaks & theorycrafting.


The members were interested in endgame, and frequently referenced a spreadsheet for HP values.


I wanted to rework that spreadsheet to make it cleaner but more detailed.


So, the first iteration (now deprecated) of my ZZZ endgame archive was made.




After about a month of constantly editing the sheet, it got tedious.


I also noticed there wasn't a place that had various ZZZ-related tools.


I wanted something more automatic, simple, and condensed.


That was when the journey of this website's development began.




ZZZ Section


In the middle of development, GachaBase was announced.


I was afraid it would deprecate my website before I even pushed it out.


So, sometimes I wouldn't sleep until 4 AM to do programming/formatting.



I eventually released Version 1.0 to the public.


It was missing a lot of things, but had plenty of features I thought people would like.


But, I should have implemented everything that's needed for ZZZ in Version 2.0!




HSR Section


After HomDGCat went down, I decided it would be fun to archive HSR endgame as well.


In the middle of development, Huroka and Nanoka were announced. (Why am I so unlucky?)


Luckily, Version 3.0 didn't take as long because I already had my ZZZ pages to help me out.




The Future


This is probably the last big version... unless I find something else to do.


I'll make an enemy viewer, maybe add missing stats to the main pages, and do translations?


Genshin content is coming? I highly doubt it though...
